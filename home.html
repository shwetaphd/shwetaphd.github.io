About

Shweta Choudhary is a Prime Minister Research Fellow (PMRF) at the Department of Chemistry, Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee, Haridwar-247667, Uttarakhand, India. Her research focuses on designing and optimizing materials computationally for energy storage and conversion applications.
