Personal Interests

Reading

I enjoy reading books on self-improvement, strength training and muscle anatomy, nutrition and health sciences, the Bhagavad Gita, and Stoic philosophy.

Writing

I enjoy journaling for introspection and staying organized, and writing blogs to share ideas, insights from books, and my perspectives on topics that interest me.

Health & Wellness

I enjoy strength training and yoga, which help me maintain physical fitness and mental well-being. I also love exploring new recipes and cooking.

Listening Music

In my leisure time, I enjoy listening to Hindi songs and soothing flute music

Mandala Art

I enjoy drawing mandala artwork, which helps me practice mindfulness, especially during difficult phases of life. I am inspired by the Buddhist message behind mandalas that nothing in the physical world lasts forever, and true peace comes from letting go of attachments and accepting change.
