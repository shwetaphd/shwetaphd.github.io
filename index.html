About Me

Shweta Choudhary is a Prime Minister Research Fellow (PMRF) at the Department of Chemistry, Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee (IIT Roorkee), Haridwar, Uttarakhand. Her research focuses on designing and optimizing materials computationally for energy storage and conversion applications.
