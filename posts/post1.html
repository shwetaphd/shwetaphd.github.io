Bhagawad Geeta Chapter 1 (Arjun Vishad Yog)

October 28, 2025

The first chapter of the Bhagawad Geeta is called the “Arjun Vishad Yog,” which means “The Yog of Sorrow and Confusion.” In this blog, I share my view and conclusions after reading this chapter.

Shlok 1.1: Deep down, each one of us knows when we are wrong and we try to neglect the Dharam because of various 'Moh' (attachments/desires) which ultimately bring restlessness.
Shlok 1.2-1.3: Whenever we look at a problem in life, it appears huge at a glance, and we start to doubt our capacity.
Shlok 1.4-1.6: We must always carefully analyse the problem first before coming to any conclusion or getting nervous.
Shlok 1.7-1.9: We should then carefully do self-analysis. Always remember that whatever problem comes in life, our skills such as talent, courage, confidence, hard work, and the genuine people around us ultimately help us overcome the problem.
Shlok 1.10: Our self-analysis should never be vague, and we must never be over confident of our skill set. We must know the reality, and we must be confident, but never be arrogant that lead to failure as we stop working on our skills.
Shlok 1.11: We must protect our precious skill/thing until we win the battle.
Shlok 1.12: We must believe that we can achieve success before actually achieving it. Use words of affirmation.
