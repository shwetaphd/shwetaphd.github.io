Research Interests

Energy Storage

Solid-State Electrolytes

The objective is to gain insights into ion transport mechanisms and facilitate the design of superionic conductors for all-solid-state battery applications

Anode Material

The aim is to understand the storage mechanism and design of carbon-based anode materials for post Li-ion batteries

Energy Conversion

Hydrogen Production

The objective is to understand reaction mechanisms and the design of (photo)electrocatalysts for hydrogen and oxygen evolution reactions

Ammonia Generation

The aim is to study electrocatalytic nitrate and nitrogen reduction pathways and tailor electrocatalysts for sustainable ammonia production
